About the TCS London Marathon

Race to the King 100k Ultramarathon will take runners around the South Downs National Park, taking in the Chichester coast and Arundel Castle as they stride to the finish. Billed as the perfect first ultra, this figure of 8 loop makes it the simplest possible format to tick off 100k. With 2 50k routes available as well, there's something for everyone!

Route information

Route: Figure of 8 Goodwood ‚Äì Goodwood - Goodwood

100k Non-Stop

Distance: 100k Elevation: 4,619ft

The SPACEROCK Trail Race features four distances, a Half Marathon, 15k, 10k & 5k race through the spectacular trails of Vasquez Rocks, PCT, and Agua Dulce trails.

This is a trail race that contains a good amount of physical challenge with elevation gains at 3250 ft for Half Marathon, 1,900 feet for the 15k, 1380 ft for 10k, and 650 ft for 5k runners. It is recommended that runners train for these elevation gains in the weeks leading up to the race.

The Half Marathon trail course consists of two separate loops, the orange loop, and the purple loop, these two combined create a slightly longer Half Marathon course at approximately 13.7 miles (22k) distance.

Going from the start half marathon runners will follow the orange course to the first aid station. From this point, half runners will take the LEFT fork to begin the purple course into the canyon section, then into the "black hole" tunnel. After coming out the tunnel half runners go RIGHT at the fork and follow the course trail markings guiding runners onto the challenging mountain section climbing three large peaks, passing two additional aid stations before returning to the PCT trail that heads back to the tunnel and returning to the first aid station to complete the purple course.

Aid Stations

There will be aid stations located throughout each route. Each station will have water, electrolytes, chews, gels, salt tablets, snacks, and basic first aid. There will be water, fruit, and food at the finish line. We recommend you carry a water bottle or a hydration pack.

Bag Drop

There will be a secure bag drop located at the start/finish line.

Frequently Asked Questions
When can I check in for the SPACEROCK Trail Race?
There will be a check in the day before the event from 1pm to 4pm at 10700 Escondido Canyon Rd Agua Dulce, CA 91350, and in the morning of the events from 5:45am to 7:50am.
Can I wear headphones at the SPACEROCK Trail Race?
Yes, but it is not recommended to ensure the safety of you and others around you.
Can I run with my dog at the SPACEROCK Trail Race?
No, unfortunately dogs are not allowed at this event.
Is there a cut-off at the SPACEROCK Trail Race?
Yes, there is a cut-off time. Please refer to the event schedule for details.
